\begin{tablenotes}[flushleft]
\scriptsize
\item The estimated mean equation is $\Delta c_{t+1}=b_{0}+PC_{E,t}^{T}b_{E}+ PC_{N,t+1}^{T}b_{N}+\varepsilon_{t+1}$, and the estimated variance equation is $\varepsilon_{t+1}^{2}= \exp(\theta_{0}+PC_{R,t}^{T}\theta_{R}+\xi_{t+1})$.
\item PPML models $E[\varepsilon^2 \mid PC_R]$, not $E[\log \varepsilon^2 \mid PC_R]$; the reported coefficients are quasi-Poisson (log link) QMLE under the exponential conditional-mean restriction.
\item The OLS column applies PPML to squared residuals from the exogenous-news OLS mean equation; $\tau{=}0$ evaluates the PPML map at the Lewbel point, and each $\tau{>}0$ cell ranges that map over the joint identified news set.

\item PPML and mean-log slopes are comparable only under an $R$-invariant innovation shape; the intercepts then differ by the Jensen gap $\log E[e^{\xi}]$ ($\approx 1.270$ under normality).
\item Zero squared residuals are admissible responses; separation/existence is checked through the positive-response rank and conditioning gates, and unresolved fits fail closed.
\item Search resolution: primary grid capped at 4000 points with a 20000-fit budget per slack; an independent Morton-ordered coverage audit (cap 8000, budget 40000) reruns every display slack before any cell ships, and disagreements demote cells to unreliable. \item Panel order is an editorial rule keyed to the benchmark crossing count at $\tau{=}0.05$, not a selection between estimators of one parameter.
\item Set cells are projection hulls of an estimated plug-in image: certified feasible attained values, inner approximations; interior attainment is not established.
\item Standard errors for the OLS and $\tau{=}0$ point columns are quasi-Poisson QMLE variances with $A = X'\mathrm{diag}(\hat\mu)X$ and score residual $\hat r = \varepsilon^2 - \hat\mu$. Four variants are computed: the model-based $\hat\varphi A^{-1}$ ($\hat\varphi = (n{-}p)^{-1}\sum_t \hat r_t^2/\hat\mu_t$), valid only if $\mathrm{Var}(\varepsilon^2 \mid PC_R) \propto \hat\mu$; the heteroskedasticity-robust Eicker--White sandwich $A^{-1}(\sum_t \hat r_t^2 x_t x_t')A^{-1}$ (HC0, and HC1 with the $n/(n{-}p)$ factor); and its Newey--West Bartlett HAC extension over 4 lags, consistent also under serially correlated scores. The reported statistics use the Newey--West HAC sandwich (Bartlett, 4 lags); parenthetical values are $\hat\theta/\mathrm{SE}$ with stars from the standard-normal (QMLE) approximation ($^{*}$/$^{**}$/$^{***}$ at 10/5/1\%).
\item The $\tau{=}0$ statistics condition on the plug-in Lewbel news vector $b_N$ and do not propagate its first-stage sampling error; $\tau{>}0$ set columns are identified-set ranges, not point estimates, so no standard error is attached (the moving-block bootstrap for set-endpoint uncertainty is deferred).

\item A genuinely one-sided identified set (one side certified unbounded at the full sample) keeps that side at infinity in the envelope; only the finite side is padded by the bootstrap critical value. Both sides expand outward from the identified set, never inward.
