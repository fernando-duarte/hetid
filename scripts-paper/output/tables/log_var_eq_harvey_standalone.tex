\begin{tablenotes}[flushleft]
\scriptsize
\item The Harvey panel is the Gaussian multiplicative-variance MLE/QMLE of $E[\varepsilon^2 \mid PC_R] = \exp(R'\theta^{H})$, fit on the fixed squared residuals $\varepsilon^2(b_N)$ at each news vector $b_N$.
\item The intercept $\theta^{H}_0$ is on the log conditional-variance scale and absorbs $2\log|m_0|$ under a rescaling of the residuals.
\item Under conditional normality $\theta^{H}_0 = \theta^{log}_0 + 1.270362845$; the general offset is $-E[\log u^2]$ for a standardized innovation with an $R$-invariant shape.
\item Zero squared residuals are handled by the direct parameter-dependent likelihood, not by adding a constant; existence is guarded by a recession-cone certificate, never by a benchmark residual crossing.
\item Finite endpoints are certified feasible attained inner values -- projection hulls of an estimated plug-in image, with interior attainment not established -- and failed fits make results unreliable.
\item Search resolution: the grid is capped at 4000 points with a 20000-fit budget per slack, and a five-start sensitivity re-polish reruns every side before any cell ships; disagreements demote the cell to unreliable.
\item Standard errors for the reference and $\tau{=}0$ point columns are Gaussian multiplicative-variance QMLE variances with $H = \frac{1}{2}R'\mathrm{diag}(\hat r)R$, $R_t = (1, PC_{R,t}')'$ and $\hat r_t = \hat\varepsilon_t^2 / \exp(R_t'\hat\theta^{H})$. Five variants are computed: the working-model Fisher $(\frac{1}{2}R'R)^{-1}$ and observed $H^{-1}$ information (valid only if the Gaussian model holds); the outer-product BHHH; the Eicker--White QMLE sandwich $H^{-1} (\sum_t \hat g_t \hat g_t')H^{-1}$ with $\hat g_t = \frac{1}{2}(1 - \hat r_t)R_t$; and its Newey--West Bartlett HAC extension over 4 lags, consistent also under serially correlated scores. The reported statistics use the Newey--West HAC sandwich (Bartlett, 4 lags); parenthetical values are $\hat\theta/\mathrm{SE}$ with stars from the standard-normal (QMLE) approximation ($^{*}$/$^{**}$/$^{***}$ at 10/5/1\%).
\item The $\tau{=}0$ statistics condition on the plug-in Lewbel news vector $b_N$ and do not propagate its first-stage sampling error; $\tau{>}0$ set columns are identified-set ranges, not point estimates. No standard error is attached; moving-block-bootstrap set-endpoint uncertainty is deferred.
\end{tablenotes}
